Next steps:

Conduct a fentanyl dependence assessment before administering:

Administer next injection if the client has “minimal/no fentanyl dependence”, see definition below, or if the client has taken at least 8 mg of sublingual buprenorphine in the last 24 hours.

Administer if they have “moderate fentanyl dependence” and are ok with the risk of some bup-induced withdrawal.

If the patient reports active use of unregulated opioids, discuss a switch to a higher dose of LAI-buprenorphine and consult prescriber if the patient is interested.*

---

When to notify provider:

Consult prescriber if patient does not meet minimal/no, or moderate fentanyl dependence criteria.
